1. Create a (small and simple) "favicon" in Illustrator or an editor of your choice
2. Save as .svg in the __ai folder
3. Optimize SVG with Optimize SVG with one of the tools below and save it in the __ai folder
    https://jakearchibald.github.io/svgomg/
    https://petercollingridge.appspot.com/svg-editor
4. Import the SVG into realfavicongenerator.net http://realfavicongenerator.net/
5. Edit options ...
6. Choose the option "I can not get rid of my website" and write "favicons" in the text field
7. Generate the codes with "Generate your Favicons and HTML code"
8. Follow recommendations 1, 2, 3 in the new page
